A little bit about Martin Garrix


Martin Garrix is a 24yr old Dutch DJ from the Netherlands, He was ranked number one on the top DJs list for 2016-2018 which is three consecutive years in a row. Some of his more famous songs are Scared to Be Lonely, In the Name of Love, and Animals. Here is a link to his actual website:Martingarrix.com
